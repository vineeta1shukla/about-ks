About me


I am a researcher in the area of applied electromagnetics.
I have worked in the areas of numerical electromagnetics,
antenna design, EMI/EMC analysis, SI/PI analysis, material
characterization, optimization algorithms, and parallel
computing. I completed my PhD in electrical engineering
from the Pennsylvania State University in 2017. Before
starting with my graduate studies, I worked in India
as a research scientist. I graduated from University
of Delhi with Bachelor's degree in ECE in 2008.
